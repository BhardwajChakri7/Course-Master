# 🎓 Course Master – AI-Powered Academic Hub 🎓

Welcome to **Course Master** – a smart, centralized platform designed to transform how universities manage courses, faculty, and learning resources. Whether you're a student seeking clarity or a faculty member organizing content, Course Master brings everything together with the power of **AI and modern web technology**. 🚀

---

## 🌐 **Why Course Master?**

Managing academic data can be chaotic. **Course Master** simplifies it all through a unified, intelligent system that enables:
- 📚 **Easy Access to Course Details**
- 👩‍🏫 **Faculty Information at Your Fingertips**
- 🗂️ **Centralized Material Repository**
- 🤖 **AI Chatbot for Instant Help**
- 🧠 **MCQ Generator for Quick Assessments**

---

## 📂 **What’s Inside?**

### 📘 **Course Repository**
- Course Codes, Titles, and Credit Structures  
- Semester-Wise Syllabus and Descriptions  

### 👨‍🏫 **Faculty Information Module**
- Academic Background and Departments  
- Research Interests and Publications  

### 🗃️ **Material Management System**
- Upload & Access PDFs, Notes, Slides, and Resources  
- Organized Per Course for Easy Navigation  

### 🤖 **AI Chatbot Assistant**
- Get Real-Time Answers About Courses  
- Powered by Natural Language Processing  

### 🧠 **MCQ Generator**
- Upload Text or PDFs to Generate Questions  
- Supports Quick Revision and Self-Evaluation  

### 🔐 **Authentication**
- Secure Login System with Firebase  
- Role-Based Access for Students & Faculty  

---

## 🚀 **Built With**

- **HTML, CSS, JavaScript** – Frontend Foundation  
- **Bootstrap** – Responsive UI Components  
- **Firebase** – Authentication and Real-Time Database  
- **OpenAI API** – For Chatbot and MCQ Generation  
- **Python (Optional)** – Backend Services for AI
